Nothing

Nothing is a song by American rock band The Script. It was released on 1 November 2018 as the lead single from their upcoming sixth studio album, Freedom Child. The song was written by the band members Danny O'Donoghue, Mark Sheehan and Glen Power, with additional writing from Ryan Tedder and Noel Zancanella. The song was produced by Tedder and Zancanella.
